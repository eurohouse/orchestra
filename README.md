# Eurohouse Orchestra
Relax in a beautiful atmosphere of high quality lounge music.

## Playlist
This music collection generally consists of jazz, lo-fi, R&B and lounge music from various artists which were discovered circa 2019.
Some honorable mentions are including:
 - Rule The World by 2 Chainz featuring Ariana Grande (2019)
 - OMG by Camila Cabello featuring Quavo (2017)
 - Think It's A Game by T-PAIN (2019)
 - Text from Your Ex by Tinie Tempah featuring Tinashe (2017)
 - LBD by Becky G (2019)
